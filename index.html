Company Overview

U-me strategic solution is in marketing agency industry, an industry that provides and widen the customer base of companies or business in Ghana andd the globe at large. The company practices the vertical management system of operation. The ultimate aim is to provide a marketing solution to business problems and provides some level leverage to new startups in their respective industry of operation

Goals and Objectives

To be the marketing agency to young entrepreneur in tertiary institution by the end of 2026. To be the best most recommendable marketing agency to young startup in Ghana by end of 2030

Mission :To grow new startup and revive old but dormant business to gain feet in the business world most especially student businesses in Ghana
